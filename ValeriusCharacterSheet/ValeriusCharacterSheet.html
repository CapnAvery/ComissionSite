"a catalogue of those who defy the grave."

Written by Capn Avery for Valerius Radomavic

The Necrarch, Valerius Radomavic

A seven foot tall corpse, animated by only the most hellish of experiments exists in a life in between the living and the dead—a makeshift vampiric Undead. His body is sustained by an insatiable quest for blood, lifeforce, and knowledge beyond measure. Valerius stems from the Radomavic bloodline of vampires, a long and proud lineage that began with Almaris by Vukosav Radomavic, the father of Vampirism. Sired by a great deceiver, he in turn embodies that trait.



Valerius, however, unlike his progenitors, does not only relish in the shadows but thrives in the spotlight. He is typically at the forefront of a charge and is considered a dangerous deceiver, capable of taking on different forms—orcish, human, elf, dwarf, and many more. Though little is known about his childhood or who he was before he became Valerius, upon closer inspection one might conclude that his natural body is of orcish descent, though after years of war, battles, and flesh modifications, hardly anything remains to support this theory.

The Black Grail

A most unholy of chalices—a golden skull hollowed out and encrusted with crimson rubies. At its center, a large ruby encases a mist-like substance, reminiscent of clouds dancing across the sky, intermingled with streaks of black. Gazing upon this center ruby, one feels an overwhelming dread.



Codex Entry: From initial research, this is the oculus of Valerius; if it is broken, the undead will rebel, and if removed, they would lose command over their ranks.



The Dreadguard of the Necrarch



The Unholy creation, it is said the Dreadguard were an elite force in the Radomavic Bloodline, buried in ceremonial tombs by the dozens. These were men and women who gave their lives for their lineage—only for their most holy tombs amongst vampiric kind to be ravaged, their unyielding service in life was not enough, and they are to serve in unlife for as long as Valerius wills it.



The Staff



Any mortal man deemed worthy enough to serve the Necrarch in life, but not worthy to ascend into a perfect unlife, is given the honor of being incorporated into the Necrarch's staff—adhering to their lord's command even if it grants them a second death. If one listens closely, one might hear a soft reverberation towards the Necrarch, though this remains but a rumor.

The Rift and its Horrors

Valerius is a capable necromancer and an even more adept magician. Having harnessed the powers of the Heith-Hadran, the Necrarch can open a temporary rift to that forsaken realm, unleashing an army of the undead. Spectral monsters—tormented souls driven to insanity—emerge, their wills enslaved by the wicked Necrarch.



The Wailing Dead



The undead brought forth by the rift amass in horrific numbers. Their forms twist and contort as the connection to the beyond remains unstable, yet they can be felled by any attack.
